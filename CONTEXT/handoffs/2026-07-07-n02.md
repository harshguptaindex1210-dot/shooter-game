## What N-02 Implements
- Scene, PerspectiveCamera (75 FOV, 0.1-2000 near/far, position (0,50,100))
- WebGLRenderer with antialias, pixel ratio clamped, shadows on medium
- `OrbitControls`: enableDamping, dampingFactor 0.1, minDistance 10, maxDistance 300, maxPolarAngle ~90┬░
- QualityPreset: `'low'` (pixelRatio=1, no shadows) and `'medium'` (pixelRatio=min(dpr,2), PCFSoftShadowMap 2048)
- 1x1 km ground plane (color 0x4a7c59, roughness 0.9), receives shadows on medium
- GridHelper (20 divisions)
- 4 POIs (Town, Factory, Docks, Hilltop) at radius 300 with labeled Groups
- Each POI: InstancedMesh ├ù 3 buildings (varying heights), shadows on medium
- 3 light sources: ambient (0x404060, 0.5), directional (white, 1.2, shadows on medium), hemisphere (sky/ground color)
