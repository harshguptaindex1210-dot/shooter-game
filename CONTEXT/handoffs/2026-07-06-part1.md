## Invariants (summary)

- **INV-1**: ≥30 fps low-end / ≥60 fps mid-range
- **INV-2**: ≤16ms local input, ≤80ms RTT target, ≤150ms cap, ≥20Hz tick, ≤200ms rollback
- **INV-3**: ≤500 KB gz bundle, ≤8s TTI on 4G
- **INV-4**: Server-authoritative; fuzz rejects all cheats
- **INV-5**: Match ≤25 min; crash → lobby ≤10s
- **INV-6**: Progression writes idempotent + durable
- **INV-7**: 4 browsers; WebGL 2 (WebGL 1 fallback)
